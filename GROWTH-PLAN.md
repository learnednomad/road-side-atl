# RoadSide ATL — Growth Plan & Targets

## Phase 1: Launch & Validate (Months 1-3)
**Goal: Prove the model works in Atlanta**

| Target | Metric |
|--------|--------|
| Active providers | 10 vetted providers on platform |
| Monthly bookings | 100 completed bookings/month |
| Avg response time | < 45 minutes |
| Customer rating | 4.5+ stars average |
| Completion rate | > 85% of bookings fulfilled |
| Monthly revenue | $8K-$12K gross (commission) |
| App installs | 500 iOS + Android |

**Key actions:** Recruit providers via Facebook groups, Craigslist, tow truck associations. Offer first 20 providers 0% commission for month 1 to build supply. Run Google Ads for "tow truck Atlanta" and "roadside help near me."

---

## Phase 2: Product-Market Fit (Months 4-6)
**Goal: Repeatable demand, reliable supply**

| Target | Metric |
|--------|--------|
| Active providers | 25 providers (coverage across all zones) |
| Monthly bookings | 400 completed bookings/month |
| Avg response time | < 30 minutes |
| Repeat customers | 15% rebooking rate |
| Monthly revenue | $25K-$35K gross |
| App installs | 2,000 cumulative |
| B2B accounts | 5 dealerships/fleet contracts |
| Provider retention | > 80% month-over-month |

**Key actions:** Launch B2B portal for dealerships and fleet managers. Add Stripe payments for cashless option. Implement surge pricing (storm mode). SEO push — rank for "roadside assistance Atlanta" top 5.

---

## Phase 3: Growth (Months 7-12)
**Goal: Dominate Atlanta metro, become the default choice**

| Target | Metric |
|--------|--------|
| Active providers | 50+ providers |
| Monthly bookings | 1,200 completed bookings/month |
| Avg response time | < 20 minutes |
| Monthly revenue | $75K-$100K gross |
| Customer acquisition cost | < $15/customer |
| LTV:CAC ratio | > 4:1 |
| App store rating | 4.7+ stars (100+ reviews) |
| B2B accounts | 20 contracts |
| Insurance partnerships | 2 insurance company referral deals |
| Repeat + referral | 30% of bookings from returning/referred |

**Key actions:** Launch referral program ($10 credit). Partner with insurance companies (State Farm, GEICO) for claims-based dispatching. Add real-time GPS tracking for customers. Push notifications for ETAs. Hire 1 ops manager to handle dispatch/quality.

---

## Phase 4: Scale (Year 2)
**Goal: Expand beyond Atlanta, build a brand**

| Target | Metric |
|--------|--------|
| Markets | Atlanta + 2 new cities (Charlotte, Nashville) |
| Monthly bookings | 5,000 across all markets |
| Annual revenue | $2M+ gross |
| Providers | 150+ across markets |
| Full-time team | 5-8 people (ops, sales, support) |
| B2B revenue share | 40% of total revenue from B2B |
| Vehicle inspections | 25% of revenue (higher margin) |

---

## North Star Metrics (Always Track)

| Metric | Why it matters |
|--------|---------------|
| **Time to dispatch** | Customer experience = speed |
| **Provider earnings/week** | Happy providers = reliable supply |
| **Completion rate** | Trust = growth |
| **Revenue per booking** | Unit economics must work |
| **NPS score** | Would they recommend us? |

---

## Revenue Model Snapshot

At Phase 3 steady state (Month 12):
- ~1,200 bookings/month x ~$115 avg ticket = **$138K GMV**
- 25% avg commission = **$34.5K platform revenue**
- B2B contracts add ~$40K/month
- **~$75K-$100K monthly revenue**

The vehicle inspection business is the margin play — $150-$399 per inspection at 18-20% commission, no emergency dispatch needed, schedulable. Push this hard in Phase 2+.
